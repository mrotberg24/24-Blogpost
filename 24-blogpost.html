The Magic of 24: How Simple Numbers Create Big Puzzles

By Mikael Rotberg | 3/31/25

Introduction

Ever wondered how four simple numbers can combine to form a puzzle that’s both deceptively straightforward and deeply intriguing? Welcome to the game of 24—a challenge where you take four given numbers and, using only addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division (plus the clever use of parentheses), try to hit exactly 24.[1]

At first glance, the rules are simple. However, as you experiment with different combinations, you’ll discover that the game hides layers of strategy and creativity. Not only does it serve as a fun brain teaser, but it also sharpens your number sense and opens the door to exploring combinatorial reasoning in everyday arithmetic.[2]

In this blogpost, we’ll dive into the magic behind the 24 Game, exploring its fascinating mathematical underpinnings, discussing common strategies, and even challenging you with puzzles to solve on your own. Let’s embark on this journey and uncover how simple numbers can create big puzzles!

How It Works

In the 24 Game, you’re given four numbers—often between 1 and 9—and the goal is to combine them (using +, –, ×, ÷ and parentheses) to reach exactly 24. Every number must be used exactly once, and the order and grouping can drastically change the outcome.[1]

This mix of simple arithmetic and creative thinking is what makes the 24 Game so addictive. Even if you’re comfortable with basic operations, the puzzle can catch you off guard, forcing you to rethink how numbers can be combined.

The Math Behind the Game

The beauty of the 24 Game goes beyond a simple arithmetic challenge—it’s also a celebration of basic combinatorics. Consider that when you have four distinct numbers, there are exactly 4! = 24 different ways to arrange them. This isn’t a coincidence: the target number itself echoes the number of possible orderings![1]

But it doesn’t stop there. When you introduce the four basic operations (+, – , ×, ÷) along with the possibility of using parentheses, the number of distinct expressions you can form increases dramatically. Even a small set of numbers can yield hundreds—or even thousands—of unique ways to reach 24.

This explosion of possibilities offers a playful glimpse into combinatorial thinking. It shows how simple rules can generate a vast landscape of outcomes, and it reinforces the idea that a solid number sense is essential for flexible arithmetic problem solving.[2]

In essence, the 24 Game is a practical way to see combinatorics in action. It reminds us that sometimes, the answer is hidden in the very structure of the problem itself— in this case, the number 24 encapsulates the richness of all possible arrangements of four elements.

Conclusion

The 24 Game is more than just a fun puzzle—it’s a window into the beauty of arithmetic and combinatorial thinking. By exploring how simple numbers can combine in creative ways, we uncover surprising relationships and strategies that sharpen our problem-solving skills.[1]
